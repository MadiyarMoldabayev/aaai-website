About AAAI

We unite researchers, practitioners, and organizations to advance artificial intelligence

Our Mission

The Artificial Intelligence Development Association (AAAI) is a global community dedicated to advancing scientific understanding of the mechanisms underlying thought and intelligent behavior and their embodiment in machines.

We strive to promote research and responsible use of artificial intelligence, increase public understanding of AI, and provide guidance for researchers and organizations.
